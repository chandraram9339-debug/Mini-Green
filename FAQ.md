# About the product

## What is Palladium?

Palladium is an autonomous trading system that analyzes crypto market microstructure in real time and executes trades automatically. It doesn’t send signals — it trades for you.

## How does the bot trade?

It monitors order flow, imbalance, CVD, liquidity density, volatility clusters, and executes trades directly. No indicators, no manual analysis.

## Does it use indicators or signals?

No. Palladium is not a signal bot — it executes based on structural patterns in market microdata.

## Can I control the trades manually?

No, the system is fully autonomous. The only actions you usually need in the app are **deposit** and **withdrawal**; **Start/Stop** for the bot, where available, follows the product design.

## Which exchanges does it work with?

Palladium trades through **Binance** liquidity (as published in product communications).

## Is it decentralized or centralized?

Palladium runs as a centralized AI engine with autonomous execution.

## Do I need to install a separate app?

No. **Palladium** is available as a **Telegram Mini App** (open it via the official bot: **@palladium_trade_bot**).

# Doubts & Fears

## Isn’t this too good to be true?

Many thought the same. That’s why we publish stats as openly as the product design allows (charts, trade journal, and statistics in the app; scope depends on server configuration).

## Is Palladium a long-term project?

Yes. Palladium is built for the long term. The team communicates plans around stability and transparency; timelines mentioned in public materials have referred to **operating at least through 2030** — follow official channel updates.

## What if Binance blocks this bot?

Palladium doesn’t present itself as a direct Binance API “bot” for your personal exchange account. Operation is described in terms of **liquidity analysis and execution** through the system architecture. See the **Technical** section.

## I was scammed before, how are you different?

We don’t take money manually, don’t promise fixed returns, and trade-related activity is designed to be reflected in the app and journal where enabled.

## What happens in a bear market?

Palladium trades in both directions where the strategy allows; performance depends on market conditions.

## What happens if everyone uses Palladium?

Markets adapt, but inefficiencies don’t vanish at once; adoption is gradual.

# Safety

## Is my money safe with Palladium?

Operations are **automated**; you initiate **deposits** to your in-app / assigned flow and request **withdrawals** according to the rules. Always verify addresses and on-chain network (**USDT TRC20** for top-ups/withdrawals in this product).

## Does Palladium have access to my exchange account?

**No** — you are not required to hand over exchange **API keys** to Palladium. The app wallet model is not “API trading on your Binance login”.

## Is Palladium regulated?

Palladium is described as a **software / analytics service**; it is **not** presented as a licensed financial institution in this FAQ. This is not legal advice.

## Do you comply with AML / FATF rules?

**Crypto-only** flows (e.g. **USDT TRC20**) are used; no bank wire KYC in this FAQ path. The project may add policies separately.

## What happens if the bot stops working?

The system is designed for stability. If **trading is paused**, your **account balance** generally remains; **withdrawal** is subject to the rules in the app (pending states, min amounts, etc.).
