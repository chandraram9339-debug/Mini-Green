# Balance and transfers

## How do I deposit USDT?

Open the **Wallet** tab, choose **Top up**, and copy your personal **USDT TRC20** (TRON) address. Send USDT from an external wallet; your balance updates after the network confirms the transfer.

## How do I withdraw funds?

In **Wallet** → **Withdraw**, enter the amount and a valid **USDT TRC20** address. Processing is automatic and may take from minutes up to a few days depending on load. **Trading for your account may be paused** while a withdrawal is pending. Minimum amount and fees are shown in the app before you confirm.

# Trading (PALLADIUM AI)

## What is PALLADIUM AI?

**PALLADIUM AI** is the **automated analytic system** behind **Palladium trade**. Open the mini app from the official bot: **@palladium_trade_bot** (start: **https://t.me/palladium_trade_bot?start**).

## How does the trading bot work?

After you **top up**, the system applies balance rules and the trading journal. Use **Start** / **Stop** in the app where available. SIB and journal sync follow server configuration after each deposit or relevant event.

## What are the fees?

Deposit, withdrawal, and any service fees are **shown before you confirm** each action. You can also review details in your **transaction history**.

# Referral and support

## How does the referral program work?

In **Settings**, copy your **Referral link** and share it. You receive **referral income** when invited users meet the qualification rules (see the referral line and rules on **Home**).

## Where can I get help?

Telegram support: **@palladium_trade_support** (**https://t.me/palladium_trade_support**). You can also open **Support** in the app and use the **Channel** and **Chat** buttons on the **Home** screen.
